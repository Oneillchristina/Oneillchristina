Senior Thesis

There’s something about the horse that captures the heart and soul of anyone who gazes at its beauty and it’s also said that a horse is a mirror to your soul. For my senior project I have decided to focus on the beauty and grace of the horse by modeling a 3D Morgan stallion based on my favorite UConn Morgans, UC Doc Sanchez, UC Nightstar, and UC Master Statesman. A stallion was chosen for it’s well developed muscles and certain look unlike mares or geldings with the key characteristics of a Morgan being compact in build, expressive head with a straight or slightly convex profile, broad forehead and most notably its large prominent eyes. The Morgan is highly known for their intelligence and companionableness and there is no doubt why the Morgan is known as “The Horse That Chooses You”.

The horse is modeled in Maya, sculpted in Mudbox, and rendered with Arnold. The model is constructed exclusivly of quads with extra detail put into the topology so that it's ready to be rigged in any application.
